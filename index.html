What this does

            
Finds the most likely tonic and whether it is Major or Minor based on pitch-class usage in your MIDI.

            
Why did it say Major not Minor?

            
Major and its relative minor share notes (e.g., C Major and A Minor). If your melody leans to the shared notes, the detector may prefer the relative key.
