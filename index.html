What this does

            
Finds the most likely tonic and whether it is Major or Minor based on the notes in your MIDI.

            
Only Major and Minor

            
For now, the detector only considers Major and Minor scales.

            
What are relative scales?

            
Major and its relative minor share all notes (e.g., C Major and A Minor all have C, D, E, F, G, A, B). Depending on which notes the melody leans more to, the detector may prefer one over the other.

            
What does "tonic" mean?

            
The tonic is the pitch everything feels drawn back to. Knowing the tonic instantly tells you the song's key, so you know which chords and melodies fit naturally.
